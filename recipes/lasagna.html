Best Lasagna Recipe

Better than Olive Garden

The ultimate lasagna is a masterpiece of comfort food, defined by a rich, slow-simmered Bolognese sauce—blending browned Italian sausage and ground beef with aromatics, red wine, and crushed tomatoes—layered between perfectly tender pasta sheets.

It achieves the perfect balance of texture and flavor through a creamy mixture of ricotta, egg, and Parmesan, dotted throughout layers of gooey mozzarella, rather than relying on an overwhelming amount of cheese.

The best technique involves layering with no-boil noodles to maintain an al dente texture, allowing the lasagna to rest for at least 30 minutes after baking at 350°F to ensure it sets properly and slices beautifully.

Ingrediants

  • Spices
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Garlic
  • Beef & Sausage
  • Egg
  • Mozzeralla
  • Parsley
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Pasta Sauce

Steps

  1. Boil Pasta: In a large pot of salted water boil lasagna noodles per the recipe below.
  2. Prepare meat sauce:Cook sausage and beef with onion and garlic. Drain well, add the pasta sauce & simmer it for a few minutes to thicken.
  3. Combine cheese mixture:Stir the cheese mixture together in a bowl.
  4. Layer & bake:Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with lasagna noodles and bake until the top of the lasagna is golden brown.
